# FlowState Group Business Plan

**Projects:** FlowState, TribeConnect, Elexa AI Trading, NoirChain  
**Owner:** Joe Robertson / Digitech Lifestyle  
**Date:** 3 June 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary

FlowState Group will be built as one connected digital business using four GitHub projects:

- **FlowState:** the main revenue product. A paid AI social media management SaaS for creators, businesses, community groups, Web3 projects and agencies.
- **TribeConnect:** the community network. A social/community platform for members, groups, discussion, ads and engagement.
- **Elexa AI Trading:** the trading education and market intelligence arm. It should begin as education, research, paper-trading, AI market explainers and affiliate monetisation, not live financial advice.
- **NoirChain:** the community payments and savings-circle concept. This should be developed last because pooled money, susu/partner-hand, rewards, deposits and payouts require compliance, legal structure, KYC and risk controls.

The best business option is to launch **FlowState first**, because it already has the clearest SaaS revenue path: AI content generation, scheduling, WordPress integration, Stripe subscriptions and pricing.

## 2. Brand Structure

**Parent brand:** FlowState Group or Digitech Lifestyle Ventures

### FlowState
- Purpose: AI social media automation and content scheduling.
- Customer: creators, small businesses, agencies, churches, community organisations, crypto projects, coaches and local businesses.
- Revenue: monthly SaaS subscription and setup services.

### TribeConnect
- Purpose: private community and social network.
- Customer: FlowState users, local/community groups, diaspora communities, creators and niche groups.
- Revenue: memberships, ads, sponsored posts, premium groups and business listings.

### Elexa AI Trading
- Purpose: AI-powered market education and trading content.
- Customer: beginner and intermediate crypto/forex/stock learners.
- Revenue: paid newsletter, courses, affiliate links, premium research, trading journal tools and bot reviews.
- Rule: do not promise profits. Use education and risk warnings.

### NoirChain
- Purpose: digital susu/partner-hand, community savings and payments.
- Customer: trusted community groups and members.
- Revenue: admin fees, transaction fees, premium group management and verification fees.
- Rule: launch as a waitlist and prototype first. Do not handle real pooled customer money until compliance is checked.

## 3. Recommended Launch Order

### Phase 1: FlowState SaaS Launch

Goal: get first paying users.

Actions:
- Connect Stripe products and payment links.
- Set live prices: Free, Pro and Agency.
- Build landing page, pricing page, dashboard page and onboarding flow.
- Add customer use cases: creator, agency, local business, crypto project and church/community group.
- Offer beta access to 20 users.
- Collect testimonials and feedback.

Suggested pricing:
- Free: £0/month, limited posts and AI credits.
- Pro: £29/month for creators and small businesses.
- Agency: £99/month for agencies and teams.
- Setup service: £199–£499 one-time fee to connect WordPress, social accounts, branding and content calendar.

### Phase 2: TribeConnect Community Layer

Goal: turn users into a network.

Actions:
- Create FlowState user community.
- Create groups: AI Content, Crypto Marketing, Local Business Growth, Community Finance, Creator Growth.
- Add member profiles, groups, posts, ads and business listings.
- Use TribeConnect as a private community for paying FlowState users.

Revenue:
- Free community access for FlowState users.
- Premium community: £9–£19/month.
- Sponsored posts and ads.
- Business directory listings.

### Phase 3: Elexa AI Trading Content Arm

Goal: monetise trading education without legal risk.

Actions:
- Build Elexa landing page.
- Create weekly AI trading report.
- Add paper-trading examples and market explainers.
- Create affiliate links for exchanges, wallets, charting tools and trading bots.
- Add disclaimer: education only, not financial advice.

Revenue:
- Free newsletter.
- Premium newsletter: £19–£49/month.
- Course: £99–£299.
- Affiliate commissions from exchanges and tools.
- Sponsored tool reviews.

### Phase 4: NoirChain Prototype and Waitlist

Goal: validate interest before touching real money.

Actions:
- Create NoirChain landing page.
- Add waitlist form.
- Explain concept: community savings circles, transparent schedules, admin dashboard, payout records and member verification.
- Add compliance warning: not live yet, pilot only.
- Build prototype with fake/test balances first.
- Speak to a UK compliance/legal adviser before handling funds.

Revenue later:
- Group setup fee.
- Monthly group admin fee.
- Small transaction/platform fee.
- Premium verification.

## 8. Compliance and Risk Notes

### FlowState
Low compliance risk. Main requirements are privacy policy, terms, billing terms, refund policy and data protection.

### TribeConnect
Moderation, user safety, privacy policy, reporting tools and content rules are needed.

### Elexa AI Trading
Must avoid investment promises. Use “education only” wording. Add financial risk disclaimer. Avoid guaranteed returns or direct personalised financial advice.

### NoirChain
Highest risk. Do not launch real pooled savings or payouts until legal/compliance advice is taken. Start with waitlist, prototype, demo mode and community research.

## 10. Simple Business Positioning

FlowState Group is a digital growth ecosystem for creators, communities and businesses. It combines AI social media tools, private community networking, trading education and future community finance infrastructure.

The first product to sell is FlowState because it is the easiest to monetise quickly. The long-term vision is to connect all products into one ecosystem where users can create content, build communities, learn about markets and eventually manage trusted community payments.
