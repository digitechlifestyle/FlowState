## Business uses for ChatGPT

ChatGPT can help with repeatable thinking work: summaries, outlines, drafts, research questions, decision criteria, and process documentation.

## Strong use cases

- Draft email replies and customer support macros
- Create marketing angles and content outlines
- Summarize meetings and long documents
- Build SOPs for recurring tasks
- Brainstorm product, offer, and pricing ideas

## Prompting approach

Give context, the desired output, examples, constraints, and success criteria. Ask for options when you are exploring and a final draft when you are executing.
