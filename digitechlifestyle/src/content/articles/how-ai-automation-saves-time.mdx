## Time savings come from repeatable decisions

AI automation works best when the work has a repeated shape: classify this, summarize that, draft a response, update a record, or notify the right person.

## High-value workflows

- Turn form submissions into CRM records and draft replies
- Summarize calls, meetings, and customer notes
- Route support requests by urgency or topic
- Convert long content into newsletter and social drafts
- Generate weekly performance summaries

## Start small

Pick one workflow that happens at least weekly. Measure the manual time, automate a version, then compare the quality and speed before expanding.
