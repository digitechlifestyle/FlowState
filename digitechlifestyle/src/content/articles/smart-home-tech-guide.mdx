## Start with outcomes

A smart home should feel calmer, safer, and easier to manage. Start with the jobs you want done before buying devices.

## Useful categories

- Smart lighting for routines and energy savings
- Security cameras and sensors for awareness
- Smart speakers and displays for control
- Thermostats and plugs for energy management
- Door locks and access devices for convenience

## Buying principles

Prioritize compatibility, privacy controls, reliable apps, and devices that still work gracefully when the internet is down.
