## The simple difference

Automation follows predefined rules. AI handles fuzzy work such as interpreting, drafting, classifying, and summarizing.

## Where they overlap

The strongest workflows combine both. Automation moves the task through the system while AI handles judgment-heavy steps inside the process.

## Example

A contact form submission can trigger an automation. AI can classify the request, draft a reply, summarize the lead, and recommend a follow-up path.
