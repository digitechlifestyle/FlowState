## Affiliate marketing basics

Affiliate marketing means earning a commission when your content refers a reader to a product or service. The durable version is built on trust, not random links.

## Launch roadmap

- Pick a niche with problems, products, and search demand
- Build comparison, review, tutorial, and alternative pages
- Add clear disclosures and honest tradeoffs
- Capture email with a useful lead magnet
- Track clicks, conversions, and content updates

## Best first content

Start with buyer-intent topics such as best tools, alternatives, pricing guides, and how-to tutorials that naturally mention products.
