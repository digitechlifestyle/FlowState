## Why small businesses need AI tools

Small businesses rarely need more software for its own sake. They need leverage: faster customer replies, better content production, cleaner admin, and clearer decisions.

The best AI tools are the ones that remove a recurring bottleneck without creating a complicated new workflow.

## Recommended stack

- ChatGPT or Claude for research, drafting, planning, and customer communication
- Canva for fast branded creative and social assets
- Notion AI for notes, SOPs, and internal knowledge
- Zapier or Make for connecting forms, email, CRM, and spreadsheets
- Grammarly for clear everyday publishing

## Buying criteria

Choose tools with simple onboarding, exportable data, clear pricing, and a use case you can test in one week. Avoid paying for overlapping products until the workflow is proven.

## Quick implementation plan

Start with one department-level problem. Document the current process, use AI to improve the draft output, then automate the handoff only after the result is reliable.
