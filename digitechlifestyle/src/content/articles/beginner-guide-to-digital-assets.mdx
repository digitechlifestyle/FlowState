## What counts as a digital asset?

A digital asset is something valuable that exists primarily online or in software. Examples include crypto assets, digital products, domains, newsletters, websites, templates, and media libraries.

## Asset categories

- Financial assets such as crypto and tokenized products
- Business assets such as websites and email lists
- Intellectual property such as templates and courses
- Identity assets such as domains and social handles

## Beginner principle

Focus on ownership, cashflow, transferability, risk, and maintenance. Some digital assets create income. Others are speculative or operational.
