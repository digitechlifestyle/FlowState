## Why no-code automation matters

No-code automation lets non-engineers connect apps, move data, and trigger workflows without building custom software from scratch.

## Tools to compare

- Zapier for broad app coverage and simple automations
- Make for visual workflows and more complex scenarios
- Airtable automations for database-centered teams
- Notion automations for lightweight workspace flows
- Native app automations when one platform owns the process

## Choosing the right tool

Start with the apps you already use, the number of steps required, error handling needs, and the team member who will maintain the workflow.
