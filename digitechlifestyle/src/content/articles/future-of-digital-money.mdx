## Money is becoming more programmable

Digital money is moving beyond online banking. Wallets, stablecoins, instant payments, and tokenized assets are changing how value moves.

## Trends to watch

- Faster settlement and lower-friction transfers
- Wallets as identity and access layers
- Tokenized real-world assets
- AI-assisted financial planning and monitoring
- More competition between banks, fintechs, and crypto networks

## Practical takeaway

The future of money rewards financial literacy and security hygiene. Learn the tools, but keep risk management first.
