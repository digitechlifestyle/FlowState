## Digital wealth is a system

Modern money is spread across banks, brokerages, wallets, subscriptions, and apps. The goal is not to install everything. It is to create visibility and repeatable habits.

## Useful app categories

- Budgeting and cashflow dashboards
- Investing and retirement platforms
- Crypto portfolio trackers
- Subscription monitoring tools
- Savings automation apps

## What to prioritize

Choose tools that reduce blind spots, protect account access, and make decisions easier. The best wealth app is the one you will review consistently.
