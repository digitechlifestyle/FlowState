## AI agents in plain English

An AI agent is software that can reason about a goal, choose steps, use tools, and continue working with less manual prompting than a standard chatbot.

The useful distinction is action. A chatbot answers. An agent can plan, search, update data, draft outputs, and report progress.

## Common agent use cases

- Research briefs and competitor monitoring
- Inbox triage and customer support routing
- Data cleanup and spreadsheet workflows
- Content repurposing across channels
- Internal task planning and QA checklists

## What to watch

Agents still need boundaries. Give them narrow goals, trusted tools, clear approval points, and logs you can review.

## Bottom line

AI agents are best treated as junior digital operators. They can save time, but the process design matters more than the hype.
