## AI changes the cost of starting

AI does not remove the need for taste, trust, or distribution. It does reduce the time needed to research, draft, package, and test offers.

## Side hustle ideas

- Niche newsletter with affiliate offers
- AI-assisted content repurposing service
- Workflow automation audits for local businesses
- Prompt packs and templates for a specific audience
- Research briefs for founders, creators, or investors

## What makes an idea viable

Look for a painful problem, reachable audience, repeatable process, and clear willingness to pay. AI should make delivery faster, not be the whole offer.
