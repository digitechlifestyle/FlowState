## Web3 in simple terms

Web3 describes internet products that use blockchains, wallets, tokens, and smart contracts to give users more direct control over assets and access.

## Core concepts

- Wallets hold keys and connect to apps
- Tokens represent value, access, or utility
- Smart contracts run rules on a blockchain
- Decentralized apps use blockchain infrastructure

## Balanced view

Web3 creates new ownership models, but it also introduces complexity, scams, volatility, and usability challenges. Learn carefully before committing money.
