## What is a digital lifestyle business?

A digital lifestyle business uses online assets, software, and repeatable systems to create income with more location and schedule flexibility.

## Building blocks

- A clear niche and audience problem
- Search-friendly content and social distribution
- Email capture and relationship building
- Affiliate offers or digital products
- Automation for delivery, support, and reporting

## First 90 days

Publish useful content, build a lead magnet, collect emails, test offers, and document every repeatable process you can automate later.
