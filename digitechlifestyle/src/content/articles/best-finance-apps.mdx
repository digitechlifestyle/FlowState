## Finance apps should create clarity

Good finance apps help you see cashflow, reduce waste, automate good habits, and make better decisions.

## App categories

- Budgeting tools for spending visibility
- Investing apps for long-term allocation
- Net worth trackers for full-picture reviews
- Subscription monitors for recurring costs
- Credit and identity monitoring tools

## Privacy matters

Before connecting accounts, review security practices, data access, export options, and whether the app supports your country and banks.
