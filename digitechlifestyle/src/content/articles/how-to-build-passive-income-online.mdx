## Passive income starts active

Most passive income begins with active work: research, content, product creation, audience building, and system setup.

## Online models

- Affiliate content that ranks in search
- Templates and digital downloads
- Newsletter sponsorships and product recommendations
- Courses and paid guides
- Automated services with clear delivery systems

## What makes it durable

Durable passive income needs traffic, trust, conversion tracking, product quality, and regular maintenance.
