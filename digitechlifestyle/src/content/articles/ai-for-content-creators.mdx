## AI as a creator workflow layer

Creators can use AI to speed up research, generate angles, script episodes, repurpose content, and manage publishing calendars.

## Useful workflows

- Turn one long video into clips, posts, and newsletter ideas
- Generate title variations and thumbnail concepts
- Summarize audience comments into content themes
- Build research briefs before recording
- Create checklists for publishing and promotion

## Keep your voice

AI should multiply your taste and perspective. Generic output is easy to produce and easy to ignore.
