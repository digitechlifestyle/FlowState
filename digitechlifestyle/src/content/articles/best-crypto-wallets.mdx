## Why wallet choice matters

A crypto wallet is not just an app. It is the control layer for digital assets, identity, and access. The right wallet depends on how often you transact and how much security you need.

## Wallet types

- Hardware wallets for long-term self-custody
- Mobile wallets for smaller everyday balances
- Browser wallets for Web3 apps
- Exchange wallets for convenience, with more platform risk

## Selection criteria

Look at recovery options, supported networks, transaction clarity, security reputation, and how easily you can separate savings from active use.

## Safety basics

Never share seed phrases, keep backups offline, test small transfers first, and avoid signing transactions you do not understand.
