✅ Monthly Review Checklist

  • Update all crypto prices and recalculate portfolio value
  • Log affiliate earnings from each programme dashboard
  • Review subscription list — cancel anything unused
  • Check any wallets or accounts not reviewed in 30+ days
  • Compare net profit to last month — up or down? Why?
  • Set one financial goal for next month (e.g. add £X to crypto, cancel X subscriptions)
  • Back up seed phrases and wallet recovery details are secure
