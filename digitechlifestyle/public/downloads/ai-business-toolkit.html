🧠 AI Operating Principles

Principle 1 — Specificity wins

Vague prompts produce vague output. Give AI context: your audience, your tone, your goal, and the format you need.

Principle 2 — Iterate, don't perfect first draft

Use AI for a first pass, then edit. Treat output as a fast starting point, not a finished product.

Principle 3 — Build reusable systems

Save prompts that work. Build SOPs around repeatable tasks. AI compounds when you systematise it.

Principle 4 — Keep humans in the loop

Review all AI output before publishing. Fact-check statistics. Add your own voice and experience.

⚡ Workflow Templates

Use each template as a starting point. Replace bracketed text with your specifics.

Content Idea Engine

Blog Outline Builder

First Draft Assistant

SEO Title & Meta Generator

Generate 5 title options (50–60 chars, keyword near front) and 1 meta description (140–160 chars) for: [TOPIC]. Primary keyword: [KEYWORD].

Content Refresh Workflow

Review this article for outdated info: [PASTE]. List: what's stale, what stats need updating, what sections to expand, new subtopics to add in 2025.

Newsletter Planner

Social Repurposing

Webinar Outline

📋 Standard Operating Procedures

New Blog Post SOP
  1. Choose topic from content calendar or trending news
  2. Research 3–5 credible sources; note key stats and quotes
  3. Run "Blog Outline Builder" template; review outline
  4. Run "First Draft Assistant" template to expand outline
  5. Edit for accuracy, UK English, and tone; add disclaimer
  6. Run "SEO Title & Meta Generator"; choose best title
  7. Add image and publish; run "Social Repurposing" template
Weekly Newsletter SOP
  1. Monday: scan 3 news sources for the week's top story
  2. Pick 1 main story + 1 actionable tip + 1 tool to feature
  3. Run "Newsletter Planner" template; draft the issue
  4. Edit subject line — test curiosity gap or specific benefit
  5. Send test to own inbox; check rendering on mobile
  6. Schedule for Thursday 8am; review open rate after 48 hrs
Product Review SOP
  1. Sign up or trial the product; document real experience
  2. Note: pricing, key features, onboarding, support quality
  3. Check Trustpilot and Reddit for common user complaints
  4. Run "Affiliate Comparison Table" template for competitors
  5. Write review (min 1,500 words): pros, cons, verdict, score
  6. Add affiliate link; publish in Reviews category
Monthly Analytics SOP
  1. Export traffic data: top pages, bounce rate, avg session time
  2. Export email data: open rate, click rate, unsubscribes
  3. Run "Analytics Insight Brief" template on the data
  4. Identify top 3 wins and top 3 underperformers
  5. Update content calendar based on what's working
  6. Log insights in monthly notes for year-on-year comparison
Lead Magnet Launch SOP
  1. Define: what problem does this solve? Who is it for?
  2. Run "Lead Magnet Builder" template; draft the content
  3. Design PDF or landing page (Canva or simple HTML)
  4. Set up email automation: deliver PDF on sign-up
  5. Create opt-in landing page; add to top blog posts
  6. Write 3-email welcome sequence (see template above)
  7. Track sign-up conversion rate weekly for first 30 days

🚀 10 Ready-to-Use Prompts

1. Instant Blog Hook Generator

Write 5 hook sentences for an article titled "[TITLE]". Each hook should use a different technique: statistic, question, bold claim, story opener, and contrarian take. UK English, no clickbait.

2. Email Subject Line Tester

Generate 10 email subject lines for a newsletter about [TOPIC]. Mix curiosity, benefit, urgency, and number-led approaches. Max 50 characters each. UK English.

3. Objection Handler

Write responses to these 5 common objections to [PRODUCT/SERVICE]: [LIST OBJECTIONS]. Each response: acknowledge the concern, reframe it, provide evidence or reassurance. Keep it conversational.

4. Testimonial Request Script

Write a short email asking a customer for a testimonial for [PRODUCT]. Make it easy for them: include 3 suggested questions they can answer. Warm, not pushy. From [YOUR NAME].

5. Repurpose to LinkedIn

Turn this into a LinkedIn post: [PASTE CONTENT]. Hook in first line (no "I" opener). 3–4 short paragraphs. End with a question. Max 1,300 characters. No links in the post body.

6. Niche Research Prompt

Analyse the [NICHE] market for a UK audience. Identify: top 5 audience pain points, 3 underserved content gaps, common monetisation methods, and 5 potential lead magnet ideas.

7. FAQ Page Generator

Create 8 FAQ entries for [PRODUCT/TOPIC]. Each: H3 question (matches real search queries) + 2–4 sentence answer in plain English. UK audience. No jargon, no financial advice.

8. Video Script Outline

Outline a 5-minute YouTube video on [TOPIC]. Sections: hook (0–15s), context (15–45s), 3 main points (45s each), summary, and CTA. Include key talking points for each section.

9. Partnership Pitch Email

Write a cold outreach email to partner with [TYPE OF BUSINESS] on [COLLABORATION IDEA]. Keep it short (150 words max). Lead with value for them. Clear ask. Professional but not stiff.

10. Content Gap Finder

I write about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. My competitors are [LIST]. What content topics are they NOT covering that my audience likely wants? Give 10 specific article ideas I could rank for.

📅 4-Week Implementation Plan

Week 1 — Set up your toolkit
  • Choose your primary AI tool (ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro)
  • Set up email automation (MailerLite or Kit)
  • Create a prompt library folder (Notion or Google Docs)
  • Run "Content Idea Engine" template for 30 article ideas
  • Draft your content calendar for the next 4 weeks
Week 2 — Build content workflows
  • Publish 2 articles using Blog Outline + First Draft templates
  • Run "Social Repurposing" template on each article
  • Schedule social posts for the week (Buffer or Postiz)
  • Create 1 lead magnet using the Lead Magnet Builder template
  • Set up welcome email sequence (3 emails)
Week 3 — Automate and systemise
  • Document your blog post process as a reusable SOP
  • Set up newsletter automation (weekly template)
  • Build FAQ page for your top product or lead magnet
  • Run competitor scan template on top 2 competitors
  • Review analytics and run Analytics Insight Brief template
Week 4 — Review and scale
  • Identify 3 high-performing content pieces to refresh
  • Run "Content Refresh Workflow" template on each
  • Add 10 new prompts to your personal prompt library
  • Plan next month's content calendar (use Content Idea Engine)
  • Review what worked and what to cut from your workflow

✅ Quality Checklist

Run this before publishing anything AI-assisted:

  • All statistics are verified from credible sources
  • No financial advice given without appropriate disclaimer
  • Content reads like a human wrote it (edit robotic phrases)
  • UK English throughout (behaviour, colour, licence, etc.)
  • Headline accurately describes what the article delivers
  • Affiliate links disclosed with "sponsored" or similar label
  • AI disclaimer added where relevant to your audience
